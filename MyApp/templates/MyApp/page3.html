Learn English

English is a global language spoken by millions of people around the world. Learning it can help in communication, travel, business, and understanding various cultures.

English uses the Latin alphabet, and its vocabulary is a mix of many languages. Start learning English today and explore a world of opportunities!

Basic Vocabulary

  • Hello - A greeting
  • Goodbye - A farewell
  • Thank you - An expression of gratitude
  • Please - A polite request
  • Excuse me - Used to get attention or apologize
  • Friend - A person you know well and like

Basic Grammar

In English, sentences follow a Subject-Verb-Object structure. For example, "I go to school" means the same as "私は学校に行きます。" in Japanese.

  • I - The subject (私)
  • Go - The action (行きます)

In English, verbs change based on tense. For example, "go" becomes "went" in the past.

Useful Phrases

  • How are you? - A greeting to ask about someone's well-being
  • My name is... - Used to introduce yourself
  • Where is the bathroom? - A common question when traveling
  • I don’t understand - Used when you need clarification
  • Do you speak English? - A helpful question when in a non-English speaking country
  • I am learning English - A phrase to use when studying English
